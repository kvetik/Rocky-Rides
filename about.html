About Us

Our Story

Rocky Rides is your premier partner for exploring the breathtaking beauty of Banff National Park. We're a locally owned and operated business, deeply rooted in the heart of the Canadian Rockies. Our mission is to provide exceptional transportation services that enhance your Banff experience. With our fleet of comfortable vehicles and experienced drivers, we ensure a smooth and enjoyable journey. Whether you're seeking a hassle-free ride to a specific destination or a customized tour, we're here to cater to your needs. We're committed to sustainable practices and minimizing our environmental impact. By choosing Rocky Rides, you're contributing to the preservation of this stunning natural landscape.

Our Vision

To be the premier choice for visitors seeking seamless and unforgettable experiences in Banff National Park. We strive to enhance your journey by offering reliable, efficient, and eco-friendly transportation services.

Our Goal

To connect you with the wonders of the Canadian Rockies. Whether you're a seasoned traveler or a first-time visitor, we're committed to making your experience unforgettable.

Our Commitment to the Environment

As stewards of the environment, we're dedicated to minimizing our ecological impact. We employ sustainable practices and prioritize eco-friendly solutions to preserve the pristine beauty of Banff National Park for generations to come.

Partnering with the Community

We're proud to be part of the Bow Valley community and are committed to supporting local businesses and initiatives. By choosing Rocky Rides, you're not only experiencing the best of Banff National Park, but also contributing to the local economy.

Your Journey, Our Priority

Our team of experienced professionals is passionate about providing exceptional customer service. We're here to assist you with personalized recommendations, local insights, and a seamless travel experience.
